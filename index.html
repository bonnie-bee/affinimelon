Chillin' as an AffiniMelon

You're a cool melon floating Town Lake on your paddleboard, just trying to have a chill day off. Unfortunately, work distractions keep showing up and harshing your mellow. It's a good thing you brought all those snacks to put you back in that relaxed state of mind. Hopefully none of them fall in the lake... nobody likes soggy snacks.

Object Of The Game

Keep chill and save the work for tomorrow when you're back on the clock!

Use your arrow keys to paddle from side to side and collect snacks while trying to avoid work notifications.
